Here, is the some cyber attacks listed below which comes under the IT ACT And Cyber Security Law

  • 01 Phishing Attacks

    Phishing is the type of cyber attack by Hackers used to obtain sensitive information or data, such as usernames, passwords and credit card details or other sensitive details by tapping a mallicious link.

  • 02 Debit Card Cloning

    Debit Card Cloning is a types of crimes which comes under the IT Acts it happens when the PIN of your debit Card is leaked to second person. after the leaking of card details the fraudent can clone your debit card and he is able to withdraw your cash from your account.

  • 03 Keylogger

    Keylogger are a type of malicious monitoring software designed to record keystrokes made by a user. One of the oldest forms of cyber threat, these keystroke loggers record the information you type into a website or application and send to back to a third party. it is used to steal the passwords and other sensitive informations.

  • 04 Ransomware

    Ransomware is a type of malware that locks target System/files. and then the attackers demands bit coins to unlock your system/files.
